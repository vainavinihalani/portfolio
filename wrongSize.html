WRONG SIZE,RIGHT PLACE

A visually immersive, experience-driven book that brings typography to life—blending history, design principles, and interactivity to help readers understand fonts beyond just text. This book will highlight typography’s often-overlooked influence, making it accessible and exciting through artistic storytelling, tactile engagement, and visual rhythm. Beyond education, it will inspire readers to explore typography as a powerful design tool, encouraging them to find inspiration in the most unexpected places and use type in bold, unconventional ways to elevate graphic design.
