WHAT'S YOUR COLOUR TODAY

What’s Your Colour Today? is a gentle, emotionally aware children’s book that helps young readers identify and express their feelings through the language of colours. Each page pairs a simple emotion with a vibrant crayon-style illustration, encouraging children to reflect on their inner world with curiosity rather than judgment. Designed to be both playful and therapeutic, the book guides kids to choose the colours that match their mood, giving them a safe, creative way to understand themselves better. Perfect for early learners, parents, and educators who value emotional well-being and mindful storytelling.
